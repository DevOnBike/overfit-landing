Privacy Policy — OverThink

Last updated: 26 June 2026

OverThink does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything runs on your device.

OverThink (“the app”) is an on-device AI chat application published by DevOnBike (Maciej Rychter). This policy explains how the app handles data.

1. No data collection, no servers

OverThink runs language-model inference entirely on your device. It has no backend servers, no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Your conversations never leave your device.

2. No network use for chat

The app does not send your messages, prompts, or generated text anywhere. Inference is fully offline / on-device.

3. Model files

You may load model files (GGUF) of your choice from your device using the system file picker. A selected model is copied into the app’s private storage so the app can load it. Models stay on your device and are never uploaded.

4. Local diagnostic log

To help you report problems, the app keeps a small diagnostic log (timestamps and error details) in its private storage. This log is not sent anywhere automatically. It is shared only if you explicitly choose “Report a problem”, which opens your email app pre-filled with the log so that you decide whether to send it to the developer (devonbike@gmail.com). You control whether and what to send.

5. Permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions: no location, contacts, camera, microphone, or internet-based data collection. Model selection uses Android’s Storage Access Framework, so no broad storage permission is required.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

7. Third parties

The app integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collecting SDKs.

8. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
